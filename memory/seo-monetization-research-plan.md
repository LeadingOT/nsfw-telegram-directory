# SEO站变现模式 - Deep Research Plan

**目标：** 找到除了directory导航站之外的SEO变现机会

**Bill的假设：** "有click进来卖广告就可以了"

**需要验证：**
1. 广告是否是最好的变现模式？
2. 还有哪些更高ROI的变现方式？
3. 什么类型的SEO站最赚钱？

---

## Research Sub-Questions

### 1. 主流SEO站变现模式
- Display ads (Google AdSense, Mediavine, Ezoic)
- Affiliate marketing (Amazon, Impact, CJ)
- Lead generation (selling leads to B2B companies)
- SaaS upsell (free content → paid tool)
- Sponsored content
- Email list → products/courses
- Programmatic SEO → specific monetization

### 2. 收入潜力对比
- RPM (revenue per 1000 visitors) by monetization type
- Case studies with actual revenue numbers
- Minimum traffic needed for each model

### 3. 最适合独立开发者的模式
- Time investment vs ROI
- Technical complexity
- Passive income potential

### 4. 2024-2026 SEO趋势
- AI Overview impact on click-through rates
- Zero-click searches
- Google algorithm updates (HCU, helpful content)
- AI-generated content policies

### 5. 具体成功案例
- Revenue breakdowns
- Traffic levels
- niche选择
- pSEO vs traditional SEO
