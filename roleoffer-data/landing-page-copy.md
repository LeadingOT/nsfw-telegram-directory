## FAQ Section

### 8 Essential Questions

**Q: How accurate is your data?**  
A: We aggregate from public sources (H1B filings, levels.fyi, BLS) and cross-reference with Carta/SaaStr benchmarks. Our estimates are within ±10% of enterprise tools like Pave, at 100x lower cost.

**Q: Do I need to share my company data?**  
A: No. We only use public benchmark data. You don't connect any systems.

**Q: Can I customize the offer letter?**  
A: Yes. You can edit all fields before downloading. Choose from 6 role-specific templates.

**Q: What if I need to make multiple offers?**  
A: Buy our 3-pack for $99 (save $48). Each offer is independent—no subscription needed.

**Q: How is this different from levels.fyi?**  
A: levels.fyi shows salary data. We generate complete offer packages (letter + equity + talk points) specifically for startups.

**Q: Is the equity % accurate?**  
A: Based on Carta's public reports and SaaStr data. Varies by stage—we show P25/P50/P75 ranges so you can decide.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**  
A: Yes. 100% refund if you're not satisfied within 24 hours.

**Q: Do you store my data?**  
A: We store the generated offer for 30 days (for you to re-download). No employee data is collected.
